I'm Vincent Tran
UX & Product Designer · Problem Solver · Collaborator




UX & Product Designer

I design with one goal in mind: To make people's lives easier. My Informatics, Human-Computer Interaction and Business background allows me to design products that prioritizes user-friendly interactions but not at the expense of stakeholder's goals and requirements. My experience conducting user research has allowed me to develop empathy for many users of diverse backgrounds, giving me unique insights on how to approach design problems and develop intuitive prototypes.

As the world progresses through technological innovations, I will focus on making these technologies not only simple to use, but also enjoyable to use. I will impact people's lives by designing with the user in mind and never striving for anything short of perfection.

Problem Solver

I love tackling design problems head first. I deconstruct each problem and analyze user pain points for every design problem I approach. By doing so, I make sure that each design decision I make is well-formed and works to solve critical design problems in products. By being a problem solver, I view failure as knowledge and leverage this knowledge to iterate and improve upon my design thinking methods.

I am passionate about UX design because I love the challenge that comes with solving a design problem. The sense of accomplishment I get when I successfully solve a design problem is unmatched. I have a desire to learn and grow as I tackle more problems.

Collaborator

As a designer, I love to collaborate with others. I always look forward to insights presented by my peers as a means to grow as a designer. Whether it be collaborating with users, clients, stakeholders, or designers; each and every one of these individuals will provide me with greater insight in how to improve people's lives, especially considering they are all people too.

If you're interested in collaborating, please reach out through the links on the right or below. This will be a great opportunity for both of us to learn and grow as collaborators.
